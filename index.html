How it works

Pickup in three
simple steps

No training required. No paper lists. No walkie-talkies. Just a phone and a few taps.

01

A parent arrives

Your volunteer scans the parent's barcode tag or looks them up by name. Their authorized children appear instantly — no paper, no guessing.

02

Rooms are notified

Each classroom sees a live display showing exactly which children are being called out. Teachers bring kids to the door when their name appears.

03

Child walks out safely

The volunteer confirms the handoff, the record is logged, and that child is marked picked up. Every release is tracked automatically.
